Sweet Potato Pie

--------------------------

By Liam McKindsey

What is a Sweet Potato Pie

---------------------------------------------------

A sweet potato pie is similar to a pumpkin pie. However,
the difference is that it has a thick layer of marshmellow
fluff meringue with marshmellows and coconut shavings.

Ingrediants

Crust

------------------
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp. ground ginger
  • 6 tbsp. cold butter
  • 2 tbsp. shortening
  • 3 to 4 tbsp. cold water

Filling

--------------------------------
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup chreem cheese, softened
  • 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
  • 3 eggs, lightly beated
  • 2 tsp. lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp. vanila extract
  • 1 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon

Meringue

-------------------------------
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1/4 tsp. cream of tartar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 jar(7 oz.) marshmallow creme
  • 1/2 cup miniature marshmallows
  • 1/4 cup flaked coconut

Intrustion and Preparation of the Sweet Potato Pie

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  1. In a food processor, compine the flour, salt and ginger to mix it. Then add the butter and shortening. Mix it all until it resemples crumbs.
  2. When processing, gradually add water to make the crumbs moisten. Shape into a disk and wrap in plastic and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out pastry to fit a 9-inch deep-dish pie plate. Transfer pastry to pie plate. Trim pastry to 1/2 inch beyond edge of plates. Line unpricked pastry with a double thickness heavy duty foil.
  4. Bake at 425 degrees F for 8 minutes. Remove foil; bake for 5 minutes more. Cool on a wire rack and reduce heat to 325 degrees F.
  5. For the filling, in a small saucepan, mix the coconut cream, brown sugar and cream cheese. Cook and stir until smooth. Transfer to a large bowl; cool for 5 minutes. Whisk in the sweet potatoes, egss, lemon juice, vanilla, salt and cinnamon. Pour into crust.
  6. Bake at 325 degrees F for 50-60 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Cover edges with foil during the last 15 minutes to prevent overbrowning if necessary.
  7. For the meringue, in a large bowl. Beat egg whites and cream of tartar on medium speed until soft peaks form. Gradually beat in sugar, 1 tbsp at a time, on high until stiff glossy peaks form and sugar is dissolved.
  8. Place the marshmallow creme in a separate large bowl; fold in a third of the egg white mixture, Spread evenly over hot filling, sealing egde to crust. Sprinkle with marshmallows and coconut.
  9. Bake 12-15 minutes longer or until top is gold brown. Cool on a wire rack for 1 hour. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours before serving.

Conclusion

-------------------------------------------------

The sweet potato pie is a great choice for any meal during the thanksgiving holidays.
It may even replace the regular pumkin pie. This pie will be a yes for anyone who has a
sweet tooth and wants a sweeter version of pumpkin pie.
